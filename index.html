Preserving the Past
for the Future

Founded in 1985, Vintage Vault began as a small passion project in a dusty London basement. Today, we are a global destination for serious collectors and interior designers alike.

We believe that every object has a soul and a story to tell. Our dedicated team of historians and restorers works tirelessly to ensure that these stories continue to be told for generations to come.
